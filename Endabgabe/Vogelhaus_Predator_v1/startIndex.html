HIT THE BIRDS


Try to hit as many birds as possible before you run out of time!

You have 60 seconds.


RULES

Use the slingshot by left-clicking. Feed the birds by right-clicking on the ground.
(You CANNOT feed them in the sky or mountains!).
If you want to feed a bird, you will lose 5 points - and if you hit a feeding bird, you'll receive 10 points.
Hitting a flying bird will get you 20 points.
And those birds that are partying and super fast? They are 50 points!


Good luck and have fun!
