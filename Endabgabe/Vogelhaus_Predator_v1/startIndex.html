HIT THE BIRDS


Try to hit as many birds as possible before you run out of time!

You have 60 seconds.

RULES

How to "HIT THE BIRDS":

LEFT-clicking will let you use the slingshot.
RIGHT-clicking on the ground will let you feed the birds.
You will lose - 5 points for throwing food. But you will get...

+ 10 for feeding birds
+ 20 for flying birds
+ 50 for party birds

Good luck and have fun!
